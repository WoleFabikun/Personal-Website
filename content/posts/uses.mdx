## What is a "uses" page?

A [uses](https://uses.tech/) page is a page on a website that lists the tools, apps, and other things that a person uses to get their work done. It's a way to share your setup with the world.

_These are not affiliate links. I don't get paid for any of these links. I just like these products and services._
